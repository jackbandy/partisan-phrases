Recognizing Reagan Chessmore


Ms. PETTERSEN. Mr. Speaker, I rise today to recognize Reagan  Chessmore for earning the Arvada Wheat Ridge Service Ambassadors for  Youth Award.

Reagan has overcome many challenges along her journey to success,  demonstrating perseverance at every step. Students who strive to make  the most of their education, like Reagan, develop crucial skills and a  work ethic that will guide them for the rest of their lives. This award  is a testament to Reagan's hard work, determination, and perseverance  at Drake Middle School and is clearly just the beginning of a bright  and promising future.

It is my honor to congratulate Reagan Chessmore on achieving the  Arvada Wheat Ridge Service Ambassadors for Youth Award.

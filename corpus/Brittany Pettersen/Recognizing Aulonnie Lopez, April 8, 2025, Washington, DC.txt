Recognizing Aulonnie Lopez


Ms. PETTERSEN. Mr. Speaker, I rise today to recognize Aulonnie Lopez  for earning the Arvada Wheat Ridge Service Ambassadors for Youth Award.

Aulonnie has overcome many challenges along her journey to success,  demonstrating perseverance at every step. Students who strive to make  the most of their education, like Aulonnie, develop crucial skills and  a work ethic that will guide them for the rest of their lives. This  award is a testament to Aulonnie's hard work, determination, and  perseverance at Drake Middle School and is clearly just the beginning  of a bright and promising future.

It is my honor to congratulate Aulonnie Lopez on achieving the Arvada  Wheat Ridge Service Ambassadors for Youth Award.

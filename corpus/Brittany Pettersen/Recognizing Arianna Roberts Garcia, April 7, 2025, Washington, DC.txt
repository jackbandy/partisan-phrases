Recognizing Arianna Roberts Garcia


Ms. PETTERSEN. Mr. Speaker, I rise today to recognize Arianna Roberts  Garcia for earning the Arvada Wheat Ridge Service Ambassadors for Youth  Award.

Arianna has overcome many challenges along her journey to success,  demonstrating perseverance at every step. Students who strive to make  the most of their education, like Arianna, develop crucial skills and a  work ethic that will guide them for the rest of their lives. This award  is a testament to Arianna's hard work, determination, and perseverance  at Arvada West High School and is clearly just the beginning of a  bright and promising future.

It is my honor to congratulate Arianna Roberts Garcia on achieving  the Arvada Wheat Ridge Service Ambassadors for Youth Award.

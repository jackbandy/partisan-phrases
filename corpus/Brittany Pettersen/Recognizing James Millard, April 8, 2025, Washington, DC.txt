Recognizing James Millard


Ms. PETTERSEN. Mr. Speaker, I rise today to recognize James Millard  for earning the Arvada Wheat Ridge Service Ambassadors for Youth Award.

James has overcome many challenges along his journey to success,  demonstrating perseverance at every step. Students who strive to make  the most of their education, like James, develop crucial skills and a  work ethic that will guide them for the rest of their lives. This award  is a testament to James's hard work, determination, and perseverance at  Drake Middle School and is clearly just the beginning of a bright and  promising future.

It is my honor to congratulate James Millard on achieving the Arvada  Wheat Ridge Service Ambassadors for Youth Award.

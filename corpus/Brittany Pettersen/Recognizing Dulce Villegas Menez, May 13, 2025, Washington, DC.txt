Recognizing Dulce Villegas Menez


https://justfacts.votesmart.org/public-statement/1691114/recognizing-dulce-villegas-menez


Ms. PETTERSEN. Mr. Speaker, I rise today to recognize Dulce Villegas  Menez for earning the Arvada Wheat Ridge Service Ambassadors for Youth  Award.

Dulce has overcome many challenges along her journey to success,  demonstrating perseverance at every step. Students who strive to make  the most of their education, like Dulce, develop crucial skills and a  work ethic that will guide them for the rest of their lives. This award  is a testament to Dulce's hard work, determination, and perseverance at  Jefferson Jr./Sr. High School and is clearly just the beginning of a  bright and promising future.

It is my honor to congratulate Dulce Villegas Menez on achieving the  Arvada Wheat Ridge Service Ambassadors for Youth Award.

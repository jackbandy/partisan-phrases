Recognizing Michael Racinowski


Ms. PETTERSEN. Mr. Speaker, I rise today to recognize Michael  Racinowski for earning the Arvada Wheat Ridge Service Ambassadors for  Youth Award.

Michael has overcome many challenges along his journey to success,  demonstrating perseverance at every step. Students who strive to make  the most of their education, like Michael, develop crucial skills and a  work ethic that will guide them for the rest of their lives. This award  is a testament to Michael's hard work, determination, and perseverance  at Arvada High School and is clearly just the beginning of a bright and  promising future.

It is my honor to congratulate Michael Racinowski on achieving the  Arvada Wheat Ridge Service Ambassadors for Youth Award.

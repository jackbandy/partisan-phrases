Recognizing Giana Padilla


https://justfacts.votesmart.org/public-statement/1691501/recognizing-giana-padilla


Ms. PETTERSEN. Mr. Speaker, I rise today to recognize Giana Padilla  for earning the Arvada Wheat Ridge Service Ambassadors for Youth Award.

Giana has overcome many challenges along her journey to success,  demonstrating perseverance at every step. Students who strive to make  the most of their education, like Giana, develop crucial skills and a  work ethic that will guide them for the rest of their lives. This award  is a testament to Giana's hard work, determination, and perseverance at  Mandalay Middle School and is clearly just the beginning of a bright  and promising future.

It is my honor to congratulate Giana Padilla on achieving the Arvada  Wheat Ridge Service Ambassadors for Youth Award.

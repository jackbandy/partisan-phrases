Recognizing Pete Rapa


Ms. PETTERSEN. Mr. Speaker, I rise today to recognize Pete Rapa for  earning the Arvada Wheat Ridge Service Ambassadors for Youth Award.

Pete has overcome many challenges along his journey to success,  demonstrating perseverance at every step. Students who strive to make  the most of their education, like Pete, develop crucial skills and a  work ethic that will guide them for the rest of their lives. This award  is a testament to Pete's hard work, determination, and perseverance at  Arvada High School and is clearly just the beginning of a bright and  promising future.

It is my honor to congratulate Pete Rapa on achieving the Arvada  Wheat Ridge Service Ambassadors for Youth Award.

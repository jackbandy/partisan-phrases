Recognizing Elijah Bertone


Ms. PETTERSEN. Mr. Speaker, I rise today to recognize Elijah Bertone  for earning the Arvada Wheat Ridge Service Ambassadors for Youth Award.

Elijah has overcome many challenges along his journey to success,  demonstrating perseverance at every step. Students who strive to make  the most of their education, like Elijah, develop crucial skills and a  work ethic that will guide them for the rest of their lives. This award  is a testament to Elijah's hard work, determination, and perseverance  at Oberon Middle School and is clearly just the beginning of a bright  and promising future.

It is my honor to congratulate Elijah Bertone on achieving the Arvada  Wheat Ridge Service Ambassadors for Youth Award.

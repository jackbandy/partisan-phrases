Recognizing Dayanara Tiscareno Escalera


https://justfacts.votesmart.org/public-statement/1691002/recognizing-dayanara-tiscareno-escalera


Ms. PETTERSEN. Mr. Speaker, I rise today to recognize Dayanara  Tiscareno Escalera for earning the Arvada Wheat Ridge Service  Ambassadors for Youth Award.

Dayanara has overcome many challenges along her journey to success,  demonstrating perseverance at every step. Students who strive to make  the most of their education, like Dayanara, develop crucial skills and  a work ethic that will guide them for the rest of their lives. This  award is a testament to Dayanara's hard work, determination, and  perseverance at Jefferson Jr./Sr. High School and is clearly just the  beginning of a bright and promising future.

It is my honor to congratulate Dayanara Tiscareno Escalera on  achieving the Arvada Wheat Ridge Service Ambassadors for Youth Award.

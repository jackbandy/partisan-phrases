Recognizing Danovin Barscocchini


https://justfacts.votesmart.org/public-statement/1689693/recognizing-danovin-barscocchini


Ms. PETTERSEN. Mr. Speaker, I rise today to recognize Danovin  Barscocchini for earning the Arvada Wheat Ridge Service Ambassadors for  Youth Award.

Danovin has overcome many challenges along his journey to success,  demonstrating perseverance at every step. Students who strive to make  the most of their education, like Danovin, develop crucial skills and a  work ethic that will guide them for the rest of their lives. This award  is a testament to Danovin's hard work, determination, and perseverance  at Arvada West High School and is clearly just the beginning of a  bright and promising future.

It is my honor to congratulate Danovin Barscocchini on achieving the  Arvada Wheat Ridge Service Ambassadors for Youth Award.

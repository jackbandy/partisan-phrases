Ms. WARREN. Mr. President, I was absent, but had I been present, I  would have voted no on rollcall vote No. 623, H.J. Res. 130, a joint  resolution providing for congressional disapproval under chapter 8 of  title 5, United States Code, of the rule submitted by the Bureau of  Land Management relating to ``Buffalo Field Office Record of Decision  and Approved Resource Management Plan Amendment.''

Requesting the Senate to Return to the House of Representatives H.R. 1834

Mr. LaLOTA. Mr. Speaker, I send to the desk a privileged resolution  and ask for its immediate consideration in the House.

The Clerk read the resolution, as follows:                                H. Res. 991

Resolved, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives       request the Senate to return       to the House the bill (H.R. 1834) entitled ``To advance       policy priorities that will break the gridlock.''.

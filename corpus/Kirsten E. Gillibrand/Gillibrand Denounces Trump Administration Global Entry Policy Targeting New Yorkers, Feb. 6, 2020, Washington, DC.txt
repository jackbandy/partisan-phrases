Gillibrand Denounces Trump Administration Global Entry Policy Targeting New Yorkers


Addressing the Trump administration's announcement that the Department of Homeland Security is temporarily banning New York residents from enrolling in Global Entry, Senator Kirsten Gillibrand released the following statement:

"Once again, the Trump administration is misusing the federal government for political retribution. There is no factual basis for this policy--its true design is to punish New York for embracing diversity and inclusion."

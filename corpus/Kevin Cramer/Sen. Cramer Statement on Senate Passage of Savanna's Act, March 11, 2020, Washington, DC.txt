Sen. Cramer Statement on Senate Passage of Savanna's Act


U.S. Senator Kevin Cramer (R-ND) issued the following statement on the Senate passing Savanna's Act today:

"Senate passage of Savanna's Act is long overdue. I thank my colleagues for supporting our legislation which seeks to combat violence against the most vulnerable members of the Native American community. I urge my colleagues in the House to move swiftly and make this the law of the land soon."

Senator Cramer helped introduce this legislation in January of 2019. The Senator presided over the Senate as it passed the bill.

Sen. Cramer Stresses His Commitment to Defending the Unborn at Right to Life Rally in Bismarck


U.S. Senator Kevin Cramer (R-ND) stressed his commitment to defending the unborn at the North Dakota Right to Life's "Rally for Life" in Bismarck today.

"I am a Senator today, in no small part, because of the issue of life," said Senator Cramer. "Thank you to those who came today for putting the most important things first and for being a light. Because of you, we are making a difference in this world."

In his remarks, Senator Cramer outlined important pro-life moments from his first year in the Senate, highlighted the importance of confirming President Trump's judicial nominees and preserving institutions like the Electoral College, and urged attendees to remain civically involved.

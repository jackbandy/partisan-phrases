Recognizing the Accomplishments of Milton Hudson


Mr. MOORE of Alabama. Mr. Speaker, today, I rise to recognize the  outstanding contributions, hard work, and legacy of Mr. Milton Hudson.  His dedicated service as a World War II Navy Veteran has made a lasting  impact on our country.

Mr. Hudson's time in the Navy has shown his bravery, dedication, and  deep love for his country. Throughout Mr. Hudson's military service, he  has had the honor of receiving the Asiatic-Pacific Ribbon, a  Commendation by Captain Strauss of the U.S. Navy, an American Campaign  Ribbon, and a Victory Medal.

I would like to wish Mr. Milton Hudson a Happy 98th Birthday and  thank him for his great service to our country.

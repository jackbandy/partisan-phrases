Recognizing Susan Harkins for 45 Years of Service with the Lower Bucks County Chamber of Commerce


https://justfacts.votesmart.org/public-statement/1694735/recognizing-susan-harkins-for-45-years-of-service-with-the-lower-bucks-county-chamber-of-commerce


Mr. FITZPATRICK. Mr. Speaker, I rise today to recognize an  outstanding constituent from my district, Mrs. Susan Harkins. It is my  distinct honor to celebrate her remarkable 45 years of service with the  Lower Bucks County Chamber of Commerce.

Since joining the Chamber in 1980, Susan has been instrumental in  supporting business and leadership development in Lower Bucks County.  She began her career in the Chamber's front office, supporting groups  such as the Golf and Transportation committees and the Miss Bucks  County Scholarship Pageant. Over the years, Susan has made  contributions as the Outlook publication coordinator, bookkeeper, and  Assistant to the President--bringing unwavering dedication and  professionalism to each role.

Today, Susan serves as the Chamber's Professional Development  Specialist, where she works toward the growth and success of Lower  Bucks professionals. Her work with the Leadership Bucks County program,  now in its 29th year, has shaped and inspired future generations of  community leaders. Through outreach events like The Keynote Morning  Meeting, Business Card Exchange, and Young Professionals Summits, Susan  has created career development and community building opportunities.

Susan's impact reaches far beyond her formal responsibilities Her  tireless work ethic, meticulous attention to detail, and steadfast  support for others have made her a cherished mentor and friend. She has  played a vital role in fostering an inclusive and welcoming environment  for all members, especially in her contributions to the Chamber's 60th  Anniversary Celebration.

Outside of the Chamber, Susan is an Assistant Treasurer of the A.  Marlyn Moyer, Jr. Scholarship Foundation, which provides scholarships  to high school seniors for their first year in postsecondary education.  She also serves as the Secretary for the Bucks County Technical High  School Foundation for Education Excellence, supporting both students  and staff alike.

For 45 years, Susan Harkins has been a fervent supporter of Lower  Bucks businesses--providing education, creating networking  opportunities, and championing advocacy on areas impacting business. It  is a privilege to celebrate someone who brings enthusiasm, good humor,  endless support, and hard work to everything she does. We celebrate  Susan on this incredible milestone and thank her for 45 years of  remarkable commitment to our community.

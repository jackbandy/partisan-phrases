Congratulating Larry Keller on His Service As Mayor of New Hope, Pennsylvania


https://justfacts.votesmart.org/public-statement/1699203/congratulating-larry-keller-on-his-service-as-mayor-of-new-hope-pennsylvania


Mr. FITZPATRICK. Mr. Speaker, I rise today to recognize an  outstanding constituent of my district, Larry Keller. Larry has  faithfully served the residents of New Hope Borough, Pennsylvania,  including as Mayor of New Hope, during his many years of dedication to  public service.

Mayor Keller has long been a committed civic leader in New Hope, a  historic river town known for its vibrant small businesses, tourism,  and strong sense of community. Throughout his time in public service,  Mayor Keller has worked diligently to preserve the character of the  borough while supporting responsible growth, public safety, and  economic vitality.

As Mayor, Larry Keller placed a strong emphasis on collaboration  between borough council, local businesses, residents, and public safety  officials. His leadership reflected a practical, community-first  approach, focused on maintaining New Hope's unique identity while  addressing the everyday needs of its residents and visitors alike.

Under his leadership, New Hope continued to strengthen its position  as one of Bucks County's most recognizable destinations, balancing  tourism with quality of life for those who call the borough home. Mayor  Keller's service exemplified integrity, approachability, and a deep  respect for local government's role in serving the public.

After years of dedicated service, Mayor Keller leaves a lasting  impact on the borough and the people he served. His commitment to  community engagement and civic responsibility stands as an example of  how local leadership plays a vital role in strengthening our  Commonwealth.

I wish to congratulate Larry Keller on his service to the citizens of  New Hope Borough and thank him for his dedication to public service and  to the community he proudly represented.

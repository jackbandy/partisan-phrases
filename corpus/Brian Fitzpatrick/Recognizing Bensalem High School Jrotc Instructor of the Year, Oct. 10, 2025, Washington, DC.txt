Recognizing Bensalem High School Jrotc Instructor of the Year, Master Sergeant Shawn Worthen, Retired


Mr. FITZPATRICK. Mr. Speaker, I rise today to recognize Master  Sergeant Shawn Worthen, United States Marine Corps, Retired, of  Bensalem High School, who has been named the Marine Corps Junior  Reserve Officers' Training Corps (JROTC) Instructor of the Year for the  past school year.

The Bensalem Township School District is proud to celebrate this  extraordinary accomplishment by one of its own. Master Sergeant Worthen  proudly serves as the Leadership Studies and Marine Instructor, and  exemplifies the highest standards of the United States Marine Corps. He  consistently instills discipline, honor, and civic responsibility in  his cadets each and every day.

This recognition is awarded annually to the very best instructors  from all Marine Corps JROTC programs nationwide. With approximately 500  Marine Corps JROTC instructors serving across the country, being  selected for this distinction is a remarkable and well-deserved honor.  It reflects not only Master Sergeant Worthen's professionalism and  excellence in teaching, but also his profound commitment to shaping the  character and leadership potential of America's youth.

Through his example, Master Sergeant Worthen continues to inspire  students at Bensalem High School to live by the Marine Corps values of  honor, courage, and commitment. These values that will guide them as  they become future leaders in their communities and beyond.

Mr. Speaker, I am proud to join the Bensalem Township School  District, the People of the 1st Congressional District of Pennsylvania,  and the United States Marine Corps in congratulating Master Sergeant  Shawn Worthen on this outstanding achievement. His dedication to his  students and to the ideals of service and leadership brings great  credit to himself, his school, and our Nation.

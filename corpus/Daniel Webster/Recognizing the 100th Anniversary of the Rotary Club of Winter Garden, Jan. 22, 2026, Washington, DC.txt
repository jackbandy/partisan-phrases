Recognizing the 100th Anniversary of the Rotary Club of Winter Garden


https://justfacts.votesmart.org/public-statement/1699377/recognizing-the-100th-anniversary-of-the-rotary-club-of-winter-garden


Mr. WEBSTER of Florida. Mr. Speaker, it is my pleasure to recognize  and celebrate the 100th Anniversary of the Rotary Club of Winter  Garden.

For the past 100 years, the Rotary Club of Winter Garden has stood as  a pillar of leadership and service. Its business and professional  members have generously volunteered their time and talents, faithfully  upholding Rotary's Four-Way Test. Their commitment to these principles  has strengthened the community and enriched countless lives.

Through community projects, scholarships, and youth initiatives, the  Rotary Club of Winter Garden has demonstrated unwavering energy and  passion for services. Its enduring support of West Orange County and  communities beyond has made a lasting and positive impact on  generations of residents.

I commend the Rotary Club of Winter Garden for a century of service  above self and extend my congratulations on this remarkable milestone.

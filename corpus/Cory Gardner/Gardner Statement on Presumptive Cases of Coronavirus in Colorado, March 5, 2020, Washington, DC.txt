Gardner Statement on Presumptive Cases of Coronavirus in Colorado


U.S. Senator Cory Gardner (R-CO) released the following statement regarding the two presumptive cases of the novel coronavirus (COVID-19) in Colorado:

"Since the first reports of COVID-19 in the United States, I have been in close contact with local, state, and federal officials to ensure the state of Colorado is prepared for COVID-19, with two presumptive cases confirmed today," said Senator Gardner. "The Senate, with my support, approved $8.3 billion today for efforts to contain and combat the spread of COVID-19 and I will continue to work my colleagues in the Colorado congressional delegation and the Governor's office to provide necessary resources as we work together to address COVID-19."

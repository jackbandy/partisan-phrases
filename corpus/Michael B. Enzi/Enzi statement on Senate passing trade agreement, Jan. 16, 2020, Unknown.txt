Enzi statement on Senate passing trade agreement


U.S. Senator Mike Enzi, R-Wyo., issued the following statement after the Senate passed the United States-Mexico-Canada Agreement with an 89-10 vote: 

"This historic bipartisan trade deal will provide much needed certainty to Wyoming industries and ensure we can maintain beneficial trade relationships with Mexico and Canada. While this trade agreement is not perfect, it will provide new opportunities for workers in our state and across the country. I look forward to the president signing it soon."

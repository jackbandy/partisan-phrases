McSally, Senators Introduce Resolution Calling for Justice for George Floyd and Opposing 'Defund the Police'


Senators Martha McSally (R-AZ) and Tom Cotton (R-AR) introduced a resolution yesterday calling for justice for George Floyd and opposing recent calls to defund the police. 

Senators Marsha Blackburn (R-TN), Roy Blunt (R-MO), Kevin Cramer (R-ND), Ted Cruz (R-TX), Joni Ernst (R-IA), Josh Hawley (R-MO), Mitch McConnell (R-KY), and Pat Toomey (R-PA) are original co-sponsors of the resolution.

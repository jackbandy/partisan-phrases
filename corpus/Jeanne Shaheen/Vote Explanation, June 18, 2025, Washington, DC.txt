Mrs. SHAHEEN. Mr. President, I was necessarily absent, but had I been  present, I would have voted yes on rollcall vote No. 313 on the cloture  motion, Motion to Invoke Cloture: Gary Andres, of Virginia, to be an  Assistant Secretary of Health and Human Services.

I was necessarily absent, but had I been present, I would have voted  no on rollcall vote No. 314 on the cloture motion, Motion to Invoke  Cloture: Olivia Trusty, of Maryland, to be a Member of the Federal  Communications Commission for the remainder of the term expiring June  30, 2025.

Mr. GRASSLEY. Mr. President, had I been present for the rollcall vote  No. 648, on the motion to concur in the House amendment to S. 1071, the  National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2026, my vote would  have been in the affirmative.

My absence is due to my attendance at the dignified transfer ceremony  at Dover Air Force Base to receive the remains of the two Iowa National  Guard servicemembers who were killed in Syria this past week. I  appreciate the opportunity to record my position.

Had I been present for the rollcall vote No. 651, on the motion to  invoke Cloture on the Nomination of Douglas Weaver, of Maryland, to be  a member of the Nuclear Regulatory Commission, my vote would have been  in the affirmative.

My absence is due to my attendance at the dignified transfer ceremony  at Dover Air Force Base to receive the remains of the two Iowa National  Guard servicemembers who were killed in Syria this past week. I  appreciate the opportunity to record my position.

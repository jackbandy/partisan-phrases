Sen. Kennedy Announces $8.3 Million in Funding for Medical Projects


Sen. John Kennedy (R-La.), a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee, today announced $8.3 million in grant funding from the Health Resources and Services Administration to support various medical projects around Louisiana, including coronavirus relief.

"Communities across Louisiana are fighting this virus with everything they've got, and this funding will help us safeguard the health of workers and families across our state," said Kennedy.

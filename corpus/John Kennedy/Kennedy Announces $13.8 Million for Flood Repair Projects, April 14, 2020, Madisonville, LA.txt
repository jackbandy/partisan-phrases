Kennedy Announces $13.8 Million for Flood Repair Projects


Sen. John Kennedy (R-La.), a member of the Senate Appropriations Committee, today announced $13,789,811 in federal funding to reimburse the cost of equipment and repair work to multiple establishments around Louisiana.

"The 2016 floods tore up facilities across Louisiana, and this grant will help pay for equipment and repairs needed to restore communities affected by this disaster," said Kennedy.

Communication From the Secretary of the Senate


https://justfacts.votesmart.org/public-statement/1689336/communication-from-the-secretary-of-the-senate


The PRESIDING OFFICER (Mr. Budd). The Chair lays before the Senate a  communication from the Secretary of the Senate regarding a message from  the President received during the adjournment of the Senate.

The senior assistant legislative clerk read as follows:

Dear Mr. President: On Friday, March 28, 2025, the       President of the United States sent by messenger the attached       sealed envelope addressed to the President of the Senate said       to contain a message from the President on the Continuation       of the National Emergency With Respect to South Sudan. The       Senate not being in session on the day which the President       delivered this message, I accepted the message at 1:17 p.m.,       and I now present to you the President's message, with the       accompanying papers, for disposition by the Senate.            Respectfully,                                                     Jackie Barber,      Secretary of the Senate.

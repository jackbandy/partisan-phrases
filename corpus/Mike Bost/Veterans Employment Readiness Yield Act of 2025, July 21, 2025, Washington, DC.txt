Veterans Employment Readiness Yield Act of 2025


https://justfacts.votesmart.org/public-statement/1693933/veterans-employment-readiness-yield-act-of-2025


Mr. BOST. Mr. Speaker, I move to suspend the rules and pass the bill  (H.R. 2625) to amend title 38, United States Code, to update certain  terminology regarding veteran employment.

The Clerk read the title of the bill.

The text of the bill is as follows:                                 H.R. 2625

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of       the United States of America in Congress assembled,       SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

This Act may be cited as the ``Veterans Employment       Readiness Yield Act of 2025'' or the ``VERY Act of 2025''.       SEC. 2. UPDATE OF CERTAIN TERMINOLOGY REGARDING VETERAN                     EMPLOYMENT.

Title 38, United States Code, is amended--

(1) by striking ``employment handicap'' each place it       occurs and inserting ``employment barrier''; and

(2) by striking ``employment handicaps'' each place it       occurs and inserting ``employment barriers''.

Mr. BOST.  2625.

Mr. BOST. Mr. Speaker, I urge all of my colleagues to support H.R.  2625, and I yield back the balance of my time.

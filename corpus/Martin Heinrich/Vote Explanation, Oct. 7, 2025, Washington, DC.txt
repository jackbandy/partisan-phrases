Mr. HEINRICH. Mr. President, on October 6, 2025, I was unavoidably  absent for the following rollcall votes. My absence was due to an  unavoidable conflict.

For rollcall vote No. 544, On the Cloture Motion (Motion to Invoke  Cloture: Motion to Proceed to S. 2882), a bill making continuing  appropriations for the fiscal year ending September 30, 2026, and for  other purposes, I would have voted yea.

For rollcall vote No. 545, On the Cloture Motion (Motion to Invoke  Cloture: Motion to Proceed to H.R. 5371), a bill making continuing  appropriations and extensions for fiscal year 2026, and for other  purposes I would have voted nay.

For rollcall vote No. 546, On the Cloture Motion (Motion to Invoke  Cloture: En bloc Nominations Provided for  Under the Provisions of S. Res. 412), I would have voted nay.

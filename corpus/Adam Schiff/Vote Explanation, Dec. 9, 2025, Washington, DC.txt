Mr. SCHIFF. Mr. President, I was necessarily absent for rollcall vote  No. 632, H.J. Res. 131, a joint resolution providing for congressional  disapproval under chapter 8 of title 5, United States Code, of the rule  submitted by the Bureau of Land Management relating to ``Coastal Plain  Oil and Gas Leasing Program Record of Decision.'' On rollcall vote No.  632, had I been present, I would have voted no.

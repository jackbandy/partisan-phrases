Roberts Applauds $15 Million in Community Health Center Grants for Kansas


U.S. Senator Pat Roberts (R-Kan.) applauded the announcement today from the Department of Health and Human Services that Kansas will receive $15,385,520 in funding designated by the Coronavirus Aid, Relief, and Economic Security (CARES) Act for Community Health Centers across the state. 

"These health centers are our first line of defense in many underserved communities," said Sen. Roberts. "Kansans rely on them and I'm pleased these grants are moving to our state so quickly to provide much needed care."

Report (H. Rept. to Accompany House Resolution 580


Ms. FOXX. Mr. Speaker, the Rules Committee report (H. Rept. 119-199)  to accompany House Resolution 580 should have included in its waivers  of all points of order against consideration of H.R. 3633 and S. 1582 a  disclosure of the following violation:

Section 303 of the Congressional Budget Act which prohibits  consideration of legislation providing new budget authority, a change  in revenues, or a change in the public debt limit, for a fiscal year  until the budget resolution for that year has been agreed to.

Graham to Serve on Task Force to Reopen Economy


U.S. Senator Lindsey Graham (R-South Carolina) today made the following statement after announcing he will serve on the bipartisan White House Task Force to reopen the American economy.

"I look forward to working with President Trump and the other members of the Task Force to find ways to safely reopen the economy. We must go on offense against the coronavirus by expanding our capability for widespread testing and producing effective therapies and vaccines.

"There are better days ahead. Americans have always overcome challenges and I'm confident we will do so once again."

Graham on Attorney General Barr


U.S. Senator Lindsey Graham (R-South Carolina) today made the following statement.

"President Trump, in selecting Bill Barr to be Attorney General, has done a great service to the people serving in the Department of Justice and our nation as a whole.

"He is the right man at the right time to reform the Department and stand up for the Rule of Law. 

"Attorney General Barr has my complete confidence."

Cotton Statement on the 47th Annual National March for Life


Senator Tom Cotton (R-Arkansas) today released the following statement on the 47th annual National March for Life:

"Tens of thousands of pro-life Americans, including many Arkansans, will march on the National Mall today to uphold our nation's highest principle: That we are all created equal, and all have a right to life. Let's pray for an end to abortion, and continue our work to protect women and babies at all ages and stages of life."

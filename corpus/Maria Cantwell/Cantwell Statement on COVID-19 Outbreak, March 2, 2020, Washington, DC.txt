Cantwell Statement on COVID-19 Outbreak


Today, U.S. Senator Maria Cantwell (D-WA) released the following statement on the ongoing novel coronavirus outbreak in Washington state and around the country:

"My thoughts are with everyone in Washington state and around the country being impacted by the novel coronavirus outbreak, and I want to thank our tireless workers who are treating patients and working to contain the spread of COVID-19. I am in constant contact with our state and local officials and ready to do whatever I can to help."

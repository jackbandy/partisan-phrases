Recognizing the Service of Chief Master Sergeant Sharma J. Haynes


https://justfacts.votesmart.org/public-statement/1692495/recognizing-the-service-of-chief-master-sergeant-sharma-j-haynes


Mr. CROW. Mr. Speaker, I rise today to recognize the distinguished  career and dedicated service of Chief Master Sergeant Sharma J. Haynes  on the occasion of her retirement from the United States Air Force.  Chief Master Sergeant Haynes has served our Nation with unwavering  commitment for over two decades, leaving an indelible mark on both the  Air Force and Space Force.

Chief Master Sergeant Haynes served as the Senior Enlisted Airman for  Space Base Delta 2 at Buckley Space Force Base in my home state of  Colorado. In this crucial role, she was the principal advisor to the  installation commander, overseeing the morale, welfare, training,  climate, readiness, and professional development of over 1,000  personnel. Her leadership was instrumental in ensuring the effective  execution of critical missile warning and intelligence, surveillance,  and reconnaissance missions worldwide. Moreover, she played a vital  role in supporting the numerous wings, mission partners, and personnel  stationed at Buckley, as well as the extensive retiree community.

A native of Greenville, South Carolina, Chief Master Sergeant Haynes  entered the Air Force in 2003 and quickly established herself as a  dedicated and capable leader. Her diverse background in financial  management included positions as Flight Chief, Enlisted Force  Development Manager, and Superintendent, with assignments and  deployments spanning the globe, from Japan and South Korea to  Afghanistan and the Pentagon.

Chief Master Sergeant Haynes' commitment to professional development  is evident in her extensive education, culminating in a Master of  Business Administration degree and completion of numerous prestigious  leadership courses. Her dedication to excellence is further highlighted  by her numerous awards and decorations, including the Bronze Star Medal  and the Meritorious Service Medal with three oak leaf clusters. She has  also been recognized with multiple Airman and Noncommissioned Officer  of the Year awards throughout her career.

Chief Master Sergeant Sharma J. Haynes embodies the highest ideals of  military service. Her leadership, dedication, and unwavering commitment  to the men and women under her charge have been exemplary. On behalf of  the Sixth Congressional District of Colorado and a grateful Nation, I  extend my deepest gratitude to Chief Master Sergeant Haynes for her  exceptional service and wish her a long and fulfilling retirement. Her  contributions to our Nation's security will be long remembered.

Recognizing Sergeant Major Harold M. Watts, III Service to the National Guard Bureau


Mr. HARRIGAN. Mr. Speaker, I rise to express profound gratitude to  Sergeant Major Harold M. Watts, III, Senior Enlisted Leader for the  National Guard Bureau's Office of Legislative Liaison. From his start  as an Armor Crewman in the North Carolina Army National Guard, he has  risen to serve as the principal legislative advisor to the Senior  Enlisted Advisor to the Chief of the National Guard Bureau.

Nearing 30 years of distinguished service, Sergeant Major Watts  served with valor in Operation Iraqi Freedom and Operation Enduring  Freedom, earning the Bronze Star Medal and Combat Action Badge. A  graduate of the United States Army Sergeants Major Academy with  advanced degrees, he exemplifies unwavering commitment to excellence.  As a legislative liaison, he has expertly bridged the National Guard  and Congress, leveraging his military construction expeltise to advance  our Nation's security.

As he transitions to his next assignment, I honor his exemplary  service and wish him, his wife Crystal, and their daughters well. His  legacy endures.

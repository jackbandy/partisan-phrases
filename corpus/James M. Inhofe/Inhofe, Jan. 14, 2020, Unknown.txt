Inhofe, Menendez Congratulate Taiwan President Tsai Ing-wen on Reelection


U.S. Sens. Jim Inhofe (R-Okla.) and Bob Menendez (D-N.J.), co-chairs of the Senate Taiwan Caucus, congratulated President Tsai Ing-wen on reelection as President of Taiwan.

"As Co-Chairs of the Senate Taiwan Caucus, we congratulate President Tsai Ing-wen on reelection as President of Taiwan," the senators said. "It is because of your work, and the determination and resilience of the people of Taiwan, that a free election was again made possible. We continue to be thankful for the strong friendship between the U.S. and Taiwan, and wish you nothing but success as you continue to advocate for a free and prosperous Taiwan."

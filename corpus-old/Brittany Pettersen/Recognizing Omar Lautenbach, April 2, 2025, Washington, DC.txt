Recognizing Omar Lautenbach


Ms. PETTERSEN. Mr. Speaker, I rise today to recognize Omar Lautenbach  for earning the Arvada Wheat Ridge Service Ambassadors for Youth Award.

Omar has overcome many challenges along their journey to success,  demonstrating perseverance at every step. Students who strive to make  the most of their education, like Omar, develop crucial skills and a  work ethic that will guide them for the rest of their lives. This award  is a testament to Omar's hard work, determination, and perseverance at  Arvada High School and is clearly just the beginning of a bright and  promising future.

It is my honor to congratulate Omar Lautenbach on achieving the  Arvada Wheat Ridge Service Ambassadors for Youth Award.

Recognizing Christelle Kumeso


Ms. PETTERSEN. Mr. Speaker, I rise today to recognize Christelle  Kumeso for earning the Arvada Wheat Ridge Service Ambassadors for Youth  Award.

Christelle has overcome many challenges along her journey to success,  demonstrating perseverance at every step. Students who strive to make  the most of their education, like Christelle, develop crucial skills  and a work ethic that will guide them for the rest of their lives. This  award is a testament to Christelle's hard work, determination, and  perseverance at Arvada High School and is clearly just the beginning of  a bright and promising future.

It is my honor to congratulate Christelle Kumeso on achieving the  Arvada Wheat Ridge Service Ambassadors for Youth Award.

Recognizing Nayeli Andazola


Ms. PETTERSEN. Mr. Speaker, I rise today to recognize Nayeli Andazola  for earning the Arvada Wheat Ridge Service Ambassadors for Youth Award.

Nayeli has overcome many challenges along her journey to success,  demonstrating perseverance at every step. Students who strive to make  the most of their education, like Nayeli, develop crucial skills and a  work ethic that will guide them for the rest of their lives. This award  is a testament to Nayeli's hard  work, determination, and perseverance at Arvada High School and is  clearly just the beginning of a bright and promising future.

It is my honor to congratulate Nayeli Andazola on achieving the  Arvada Wheat Ridge Service Ambassadors for Youth Award.

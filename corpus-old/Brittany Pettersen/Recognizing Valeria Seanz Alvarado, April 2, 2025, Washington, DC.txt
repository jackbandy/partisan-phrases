Recognizing Valeria Seanz Alvarado


Ms. PETTERSEN. Mr. Speaker, I rise today to recognize Valeria Seanz  Alvarado for earning the Arvada Wheat Ridge Service Ambassadors for  Youth Award.

Valeria has overcome many challenges along her journey to success,  demonstrating perseverance at every step. Students who strive to make  the most of their education, like Valeria, develop crucial skills and a  work ethic that will guide them for the rest of their lives. This award  is a testament to Valeria's hard work, determination, and perseverance  at Arvada High School and is clearly just the beginning of a bright and  promising future.

It is my honor to congratulate Valeria Seanz Alvarado on achieving  the Arvada Wheat Ridge Service Ambassadors for Youth Award.

Recognizing Rachelle Martinez Marentes


Ms. PETTERSEN. Mr. Speaker, I rise today to recognize Rachelle  Martinez Marentes for earning the Arvada Wheat Ridge Service  Ambassadors for Youth Award.

Rachelle has overcome many challenges along her journey to success,  demonstrating perseverance at every step. Students who strive to make  the most of their education, like Rachelle, develop crucial skills and  a work ethic that will guide them for the rest of their lives. This  award is a testament to Rachelle's hard work, determination, and  perseverance at Arvada High School and is clearly just the beginning of  a bright and promising future.

It is my honor to congratulate Rachelle Martinez Marentes on  achieving the Arvada Wheat Ridge Service Ambassadors for Youth Award.

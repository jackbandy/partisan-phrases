Vote Explanation


Mrs. SHAHEEN. Mr. President, I was necessarily absent, but had I been  present, I would have voted yes on rollcall vote No. 210 on the Cloture  Motion (Motion to Invoke Cloture: Lt. Gen. John D. Caine (Retired) to  be Major General in the Regular Air Force).

I was necessarily absent, but had I been present, I would have voted  yes on rollcall vote No. 211 on the Nomination (Confirmation: Lt. Gen.  John D. Caine (Retired) to be Major General in the Regular Air Force).

I was necessarily absent, but had I been present, I would have voted  yes on rollcall vote No. 212 on the Cloture Motion (Motion to Invoke  Cloture: Lt. Gen. John D. Caine (Retired) to be General and Chairman of  the Joint Chiefs of Staff).

I was necessarily absent, but had I been present, I would have voted  yes on rollcall vote No. 213 On the Nomination (Confirmation: Lt. Gen.  John D. Caine (Retired) to be General and Chairman of the Joint Chiefs  of Staff).

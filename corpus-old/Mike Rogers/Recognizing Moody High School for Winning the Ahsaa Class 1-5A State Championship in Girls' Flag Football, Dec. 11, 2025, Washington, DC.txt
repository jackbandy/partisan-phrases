Recognizing Moody High School for Winning the Ahsaa Class 1-5A State Championship in Girls' Flag Football


Mr. ROGERS of Alabama. Mr. Speaker, I rise today to recognize the  Moody High School girls' flag football team for winning the Alabama  High School Athletic Association (AHSAA) 1-5A State Championship.

At Protective Stadium in Birmingham, Alabama, on December 3, 2025,  the Moody flag football team, with a record of 22-3, secured the  school's first state championship in flag football over Montgomery  Catholic School with a score of 28-17.

The championship team from Moody, Alabama, is led by Coach Wren Moore  and is the school's first flag football state title.

Mr. Speaker, please join me in recognizing the Moody girls' flag  football team, Coach Moore and the coaching staff, the faculty and fans  on this exciting win for the school. I congratulate them and Go Blue  Devils.

Recognizing E.J. Basler Co.


Mr. QUIGLEY. Mr. Speaker, I rise today to recognize E.J. Basler Co.  in Schiller Park, Illinois.

E.J. Basler Co., a family-owned manufacturing company, has been based  in Schiller Park since 1957. The company was built on the core values  of quality and customer service, founded by Erv and Anne Basler,  children of immigrants. Now in its third generation of family  leadership, E.J. Basler Co. is run by Dennis and Brian Basler. Their  ongoing commitment to responsive service and high-quality manufacturing  has supported strong, long-term relationships with clients and vendors  in Illinois' fifth district and across the globe. E.J. Basler Co. has  achieved lasting success by staying true to its original values while  evolving with current times.

E.J. Basler Co. offers precision machinery services that leverage  different techniques, cutting-edge technology, and expertise to create  the most cost-effective solutions for customers. They manufacture from  iconel, brass, steel, plastic, and copper. Manufacturing is crucial to  the global economy, and E.J. Basler Co. delivers high-quality solutions  across industries, working with the best of agriculture, plumbing,  automotive, industrial, defense, and medical companies. The close  proximity to E.J. Basler Co. and its valuable services offer companies  in Illinois' fifth district a competitive advantage and drive economic  development within the district.

Mr. Speaker, I ask my colleagues to join me in celebrating the  longstanding dedication and success of E.J. Basler Co. I am honored to  have such an exceptional company in my district.

Medicaid Cuts Mean Hospital Closures


Mr. KHANNA. Mr. Speaker, the Republican budget calls for a trillion  dollars in Medicaid cuts to pay for tax breaks for the very wealthy.

Mr. Speaker, 83 percent of the tax breaks go to 1 percent of  Americans. What do these Medicaid cuts mean? It means the closure of  hospitals and seniors denied coverage in long-term care.

Elon Musk talks about reducing deficits, but this bill would add $2.8  trillion to the deficit, taking it to $24.9 trillion.

Here is what is going on in the Republican family: While Musk and  DOGE scrounge around for change under the couch, Speaker Johnson is  running the credit card, bankrupting our Nation.

They are taking away your Medicaid to line the pockets of the  wealthiest Americans who are controlling our government.

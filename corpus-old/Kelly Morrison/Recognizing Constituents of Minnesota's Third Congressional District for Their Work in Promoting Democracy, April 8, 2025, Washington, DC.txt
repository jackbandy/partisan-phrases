Recognizing Constituents of Minnesota's Third Congressional District for Their Work in Promoting Democracy


Ms. MORRISON. Mr. Speaker, I rise today to recognize several  constituents in Minnesota's Third Congressional District. Their  dedication to promoting democracy is to be commended. I recognize  Sharon Billings, Caitlin Cahill, Keith Engel, Aditi Ginuga, Anne  Leland, Cathy Olson, Dave Olson, Paula Ramaley, Cole Roepke, Adaline  Shinkle, Brad Shinkle, Diane Sjolander, and Anita Smithson.

The people of Minnesota's Third Congressional District are fortunate  to have them as neighbors, friends, and community members.

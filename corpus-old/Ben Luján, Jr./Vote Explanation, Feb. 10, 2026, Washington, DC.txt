Vote Explanation


Mr. LUJAN. Mr. President, I was unavailable for rollcall vote No. 21,  on Motion to Invoke Cloture on the nomination of David Clay Fowlkes to  be United States District Judge for the Western District of Arkansas.  Had I been present, I would have voted nay.

I was unavailable for rollcall vote No. 22, on Motion to Invoke  Cloture on David Clay Fowlkes to be United States District Judge for  the Western District of Arkansas. Had I been present, I would have  voted nay.

I was unavailable for rollcall vote No. 23, on Motion to Invoke  Cloture on Nicholas Jon Ganjei, of Texas, to be United States District  Judge for the Southern District of Texas. Had I been present, I would  have voted nay.

I was unavailable for rollcall vote No. 24, on the confirmation on  Nicholas Jon Ganjei, of Texas, to be United States District Judge for  the Southern District of Texas. Had I been present, I would have voted  nay.

I was unavailable for rollcall vote No. 25, on Motion to Invoke  Cloture on Aaron Christian Peterson, to be United States District Judge  for the District of Alaska. Had I been present, I would have voted nay.
